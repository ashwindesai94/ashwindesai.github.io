About Me

Dr. Ashwin Desai is an alumnus of IIT Madras (Ph.D., M.S.) and Associate Professor at Dayananda Sagar University. He co-leads SynTech Bio Solutions, an interdisciplinary lab focusing on AI in drug discovery, nanotechnology, and biomedical sensors.

Research Interests: AI for drug discovery, PEG-PLGA nanoparticles, biosensors for bacterial/cancer detection, high-voltage protein extraction.
